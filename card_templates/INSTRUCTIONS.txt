
Card Template Collection Instructions:
=====================================

To enable card recognition, you need to collect card templates from PokerStars.

1. Start PokerStars and join a play-money table
2. Wait for cards to be dealt to you
3. Take screenshots when you have different cards
4. Use image editing software to crop individual cards
5. Save them in the card_templates/ folder with these names:

Format: [rank][suit].png
Examples: Ah.png (Ace of Hearts), Ts.png (Ten of Spades)

Ranks: 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, T, J, Q, K, A
Suits: h (hearts), d (diamonds), c (clubs), s (spades)

Tips:
- Crop cards to show just the card face (no background)
- Keep consistent sizing (recommended: 60x80 pixels)
- Use PNG format for transparency support
- Ensure good contrast and clarity
